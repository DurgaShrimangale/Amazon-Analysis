Project Overview
The Amazon Product Sales Analysis project leverages Power BI to transform raw e-commerce sales data into actionable business insights.
By visualizing trends, product performance, and regional sales patterns, the dashboard helps stakeholders make informed decisions
to boost revenue, optimize inventory, and tailor marketing strategies.

Objectives
- Analyze sales performance across product categories, regions, and time periods.
- Identify top-selling products, underperforming segments, and growth opportunities.
- Evaluate the impact of discounts, pricing, and seasonality on sales.
- Create an interactive, shareable dashboard for business teams.
 
 Bullet Points
- Goal: Analyze Amazon sales performance.
- Tools: Power BI, DAX.
- Insights: Electronics dominate; discounts boost sales.
- Outcome: Better decisions for growth.

 Dataset- Source:
- Amazon sales records (CSV/Excel).
- Key Fields:
    - Product details: ID, category, subcategory, price.
    - Sales metrics: revenue, units sold, profit.
    - Geography: country, state, city.
    - Time: order date, month, year.

 Technical Summary
 
 "Built a Power BI dashboard with sales KPIs, time-series trends, and geographic maps.
  Used DAX for calculations (revenue, YoY growth). Cleaned data via Power Query."
